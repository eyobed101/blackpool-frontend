2. TYPES

2.1. TYPE OF BETS

2.1.1.AKO bet – is a bet that consists of one or more betting markets at the same time. The final odds in an AKO bet is the result of the multiplication of all selected odds of all betting markets on the ticket. The final odds are always rounded to two decimal places. When the third decimal place shall be the number 5, the final odds are rounded up (e.g. 3,125 will be 3,13). The ticket is considered a winning ticket only when all betting markets are correctly predicted.

2.2. TYPE OF TICKETS

2.2.1. Prematch Ticket - is a ticket that consists of one or more prematch bets.

2.2.2. Live Ticket - is a ticket that consists of one or more Live bets.

2.2.3. Mix Ticket - is a ticket that consists of a combination of one or more live bets and one or more prematch bets.

2.3. TYPE OF BETTING MARKETS

2.3.1. Betting markets with two possible outcomes (e.g. tennis, volleyball).

2.3.2. Betting markets with three possible outcomes (e.g. football, ice hockey).

2.3.3. Betting markets on victory or placement of individuals or teams (e.g. Formula1).

2.3.4. Other betting markets – it is possible to use, among others, the following types of betting opportunities on tickets: the bet on an exact outcome, the bet on placing, the bet on the course of a match, the bet on the first set, the bet on handicap, etc.

2.3.5. LIVE betting markets – means betting opportunities on a sports match for which it is possible to accept bets during its course. They are marked as “Live Betting” in the offer of the company. Live Betting may not be cancelled after the bets are placed.

3.4. OFFICIAL RESULT AND BET SETTLEMENT

3.4.1. The company draws official results from official sources and can publish them on its website. In a case that a published official result is incorrect, the right of the client to claim the winning is not affected as long as the client correctly determined the result of the outcome.

3.4.2. In an event with a previously determined playing time (e.g. football), the official result is considered to be the one that was achieved on the playing ground, if these conditions are met: the match was properly played to the end within the whole playing time determined (from the 1st minute, without any extra time, penalty kicks, etc.) and it was properly ended.

3.4.3. In case an event is not completed, only bets where the outcome of the bet is unambiguous at the time of the suspension will be settled (e.g. half-time result, team to score a goal, number of goals, etc.)

3.4.4. Own goals are not taken into account for settlement purposes in the “anytime goalscorer” bet type.

3.4.5. Qualifications are considered to be a part of the main race. Therefor if a competitor (or a team) takes part in the qualification, but does not qualify for the main race, bets placed on the placement in the main race are considered lost.

3.4.6. Bets on tennis are not considered void if the event is taking place on a different surface that was assumed.

3.4.7. If a match ends in the standard playing time and thereafter is forfeited (due to disqualification, doping, etc.) or is replayed, the result achieved on the playing ground is applied. Similar procedures are applied in events where the playing time is not predetermined.

3.4.8. In the event that a match or competition takes place for more than 3 days from the original published start, the company reserves the right to cancel bets placed on that match or competition. In case the event starts before the originally scheduled date or time, all bets placed before the actual start will stand. Bets placed after the actual start of the match are refunded (odds 1.00).

3.4.9. In case a baseball game is for whatever reason not being played at the day published on the offer, all bets are void. If two teams are playing two games in a row in one day, the subject-matter of the bet shall always be only the first one.

3.4.10. Retirement or disqualification of a listed (odds were published) competitor or a team (e.g. Formula 1) in an event makes the bet lost. If a competitor or team does not participate in the competition at all, bets will be refunded (odds 1.00).

3.4.11. Evaluation of tennis betting markets - tiebreak or super tiebreak counts as one game. Super tiebreak is counted as one set.

3.4.12. In case one of the players retired, for example in a tennis match, all betting selections for which the result is already known are evaluated. Other bets, including the bet on the winner of the match, will be refunded (odds 1.00).

3.4.13. A complaint directed against any obvious inaccuracy in the published outcome shall be acknowledged. In such case payouts according to the right result will be initiated as soon as the result will be corrected.

3.4.14. After the official result was announced and a protest settled, no other corrections of outcomes (proved doping, etc.) will be taken into account.

3.4.15. If a race event is interrupted or shortened (e.g. Formula 1), the achieved outcome and the overall placings as they were determined by the authorized body (except for predefined playing time events) shall be applied.

3.4.16. If more than one race is taking part in one place in a short time period, the subject-matter of the bets shall always be the first realized race.

3.4.17. If a minor error has occurred while entering betting markets into the application such as wrong name of a competitor, team or competition, all bets remain valid if this mistake does not mislead the client with the nature of the bet.

3.4.18. In the case of LIVE bets, the official result is the result published by the company. Bets, which will not be able to evaluate of bets will be considered invalid and therefore will be refunded (odds 1.00).

3.4.19. In case there is an obvious odds error (odds displayed are different by more than 0,8 to the market average), bet will be evaluated in the odds 1.00.

3.4.20. Goalscorer bets (First goalscorer, Anytime goalscorer, Last goalscorer) are only valid if the player is in the starting lineup. Non fielders, bench players and substitutes will be refunded (odds 1.00).

3.5.1. Clients are eligible for the winnings if all conditions below are met:

- The outcome of the bet was correctly determined.

3.6.1. Payouts will begin as soon as the company publishes the official results of all bets on the ticket.

3.6.2. If the winning amount is not available, the company sets a deadline of 30 days for making the payments. This time period begins when the last bet on the ticket is evaluated. The bet stored in the database in the central server of the company that has been evaluated as correct is decisive for the assessment of the entitlement to the winnings.

3.6.3. In case a payout is credited by mistake to client’s account (e.g. due to an incorrect result) the company reserves the right to deduct this payout from the account without the client’s permission. If this leads to a negative balance on the client’s account, the client is obliged to deposit required amount of money in order to have a positive balance on the account again.

3.7. REFUND OF BETS

3.7.1. Bets shall be refunded and considered invalid and void in the following cases:

3.7.1.1. If a match with predefined playing time, or other predefined criterion (number of sets, etc.) is not played or is cancelled before the regular end due to unpredictable circumstances, such as mist, electricity failure, audience behavior, etc. Bets on all occasions are void and will be refunded (odds 1.00), except for bets where the result is known (for example, "Who will score the first goal").

3.7.1.2. In case the home team is wrongly identified (e.g. in a football game). In this case all bets will be void regardless to the outcome. This rule does not apply when event is taking place on a neutral ground. Also, when an event is taking part “behind closed doors” with no audience.

3.7.1.3. When a betting market is entered incorrectly by the company or when the entering has lost its meaning due to “force majeure” (a change in the rules of the competition, etc.), it shall be considered void and the bet shall be refunded (odds 1.00).

3.7.1.4. The event is not played within 3 days after the original scheduled start. In this case, all bets are void and will be refunded (odds 1.00).

3.7.1.5. Bets on the better placing bet type are refunded when both of the competitors will not classify or will end in the same place.

3.7.1.6. If the bet is placed after the official start of the game. This rule does not apply for LIVE bets.

3.7.1.7. If the company set incorrect goal line, handicap line, etc.

3.7.2. AKO bets are considered invalid when all bet selections are invalid. In other cases, AKO bets remain valid and all invalid selections will be eliminated from the bet and the winning (if any) shall be calculated without the odds of these betting markets that will be void (odds of 1.00).

3.7.3. A bet was by mistake of the company placed on an event where the outcome was known in advance or was influenced by the organizer of the sports event, the client, or other persons.
